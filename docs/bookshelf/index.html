This is my bookshelf. It is simply a semi-up-to-date list of the books I own, many of which I haven't read. I unabashedly ripped off this concept from Patrick Collison. "I believe that, confronted by a vast array of books, anyone will be seized by the anguish of learning, and will inevitably lapse into asking the question that expresses his torment and his remorse" - Umberto Eco. Welp, that's the idea.
